How it works

Not a tracker.
A verification engine.

Most IoT platforms collect data. Boson does something different — it understands what the data means.

When a road roller moves across a construction site, Boson isn't just recording GPS coordinates. It's counting every compaction pass per chainage, validating that the equipment completed the required sequence, and cross-referencing the material layer specification with the observed machine behaviour. If something is wrong — a pass missed, equipment idle when it should be working — Boson flags it before the layer is sealed and the mistake becomes expensive.

This is physics-based AI: models trained on the mechanics of compaction, material fatigue, structural load, and equipment lifecycle — not on generic datasets, but on the actual physics of the work being monitored.

Common questions

to evaluate Boson.

Don't see your question? Talk to our team — we typically respond within 2 hours.

What devices does Boson support?+
Boson ships with codecs for 20+ OEM telematics manufacturers — including TATA Advance, 808GPS, Mahindra, Fleetedge, TrackStar, and custom TCP/MQTT devices. If your device isn't in the library, we build the codec in 5 business days. We support HTTP push, MQTT, raw TCP, and pull API protocols.
Does my team need to replace existing hardware?+
No. Boson is designed to connect to the devices you already have in the field. Bring your existing GPS trackers, telematics units, IoT sensors, or custom-built gateways. Boson normalises the signal — you don't change the hardware.
How long does it take to go live?+
Typically 7 days from signed agreement to live monitoring. That includes device onboarding, codec configuration, workgroup setup, and dashboard access for your team.
Is Boson data secure and isolated by organisation?+
Yes. Boson is a fully multi-tenant platform. Each organisation's data is isolated at the workgroup level — schema-separated, access-controlled, and cascade-deleted on offboarding. Data in transit is Cloudflare-tunnelled. Data at rest uses Fernet encryption for credentials and AWS-grade storage for telemetry.
How does Boson connect to the Cortex dashboard?+
Boson normalises incoming telemetry and publishes structured events which Cortex consumes in real time. Every verified field event appears in the Cortex dashboard as a structured, timestamped record. No manual handoff. The pipeline is automatic.
Can Boson handle multiple sites and multiple countries?+
Yes. The platform is built for multi-tenant, multi-geography deployment. We have customers running simultaneous operations across India, Australia, and Africa on the same Boson instance. Devices are provisioned by workgroup, and monitoring boundaries are configurable per site.
